🎮 GameHub: Lightweight System Monitor & Overlay
Welcome to GameHub, a sleek, minimalistic system monitoring utility designed for gamers, developers, and power users. GameHub provides real-time insights into your system's performance without cluttering your screen or interrupting your workflow.

Whether you're tracking your ping during a competitive match or keeping an eye on background memory hogs while coding, GameHub has you covered.

✨ Features
Real-Time Hardware Metrics: Live tracking for CPU, GPU, and RAM usage to ensure your system is running optimally.

Network Monitoring: Keep an eye on your connection with live Ping (ms) and active Download/Upload speeds.

Process Tracker: Instantly view the "Top RAM Consumers" to identify exactly which background processes (like browsers or host services) are using up your memory.

Built-in Session/Work Timer: Easily track your active gaming sessions or focused work/study intervals directly from the widget.

Minimalist, Dark-Themed UI: A clean, unobtrusive interface designed to blend seamlessly into your desktop environment.
